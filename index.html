How to Play

🎯

Fill the Grid

Place digits 1–9 so every row, column, and 3×3 box contains each number exactly once.

✏️

Use Notes

Toggle Notes Mode to pencil in candidate numbers in any cell — great for tricky spots.

💡

Get Hints

Stuck? Use one of your 3 hints to reveal the correct value in any empty cell.

⏱️

Beat Your Time

Finish fast with few mistakes for the highest score. Your best times go on the leaderboard.
